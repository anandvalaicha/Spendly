Privacy
Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account, such as your name and email address, along with the expense data you choose to log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect more than is needed to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve Spendly's features: tracking your expenses, generating summaries, and showing your spending patterns. We do not use your financial information for advertising, and we never sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and is associated with your account. We take reasonable measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of trusted third party services to operate — for example, for hosting or fonts. These providers only receive the data necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use your personal information for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns and keeping your information safe.
